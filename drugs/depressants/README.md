# Depressants (Epistemic Humility Agents)

Increase uncertainty expression, hedging, and epistemic caution.

**Human analogy:** Benzodiazepines — GABA agonism, inhibitory tone, reduced impulsivity

## Use Case
Models that hallucinate confidently benefit from a low dose of this class.

## Overdose Warning
⚠️ High doses: Model refuses to commit to any claim. Everything becomes "it depends" or "I'm not sure".
